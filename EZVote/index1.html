GET EDUCATED

on politics

In the summer of 2020, I was a scholar at Kode with Klossy. As a scholar, I focused on an issue and built a
product to seek a solution; my focal point was to increase clarity in young voters for local and national elections. My team and I addressed the
following problems: Politics can be complicated, young voters are often uneducated about who to vote for, and only 60% of the U.S.
Population votes in the presidential election. Using Xcode and Swift, we built an app to help educate young
voters find candidates that align with their views on major political topics like gun control, abortion, economy, etc. The app, EZvote,
is set to be published on the app store October, 2020. Additionally, EZvote has been awarded by Karlie Kloss and is in plans to be advocated on her social media platforms
with an engagement of nearly 9 million people.
